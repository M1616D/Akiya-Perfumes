FAQ

Your Questions

Everything you need to know about Akiya Perfumes.

How long do Akiya perfumes last?
Our perfumes are crafted with high concentrations of premium oils, ensuring they last between 8 to 12 hours on skin and even longer on clothing. The longevity varies by fragrance family — oriental and woody scents tend to last the longest.
Are all products 100% original?
Absolutely. We source directly from certified manufacturers and distributors. Every bottle comes with a unique authenticity code that you can verify on our website.
Do you deliver across the country?
Yes, we deliver nationwide with secure, discreet packaging. Orders are processed within 24 hours and typically arrive within 2-5 business days. We also offer express shipping for urgent orders.
How can I place an order?
You can place an order directly through our website, or send us a message on WhatsApp with your desired products. We'll guide you through the process and confirm your order within minutes.
Do you offer samples or testers?
Yes, we offer a sample discovery set that includes 5 of our most popular fragrances. It's the perfect way to find your signature scent before committing to a full bottle.
